Vision

In a world where the environment is becoming more and more polluted and it is slowly dying, we believe that mainly small steps can start the movement towards keeping nature alive. Taking action breaks the cycle and sustainability can become a way of lifestyle to all!


Our Brand

Almost all care products goods are sold in plastic packages that are often non-recyclable. Equilibre has an easy solution for people to integrate plastic-free products into their life. Introducing the new toothpaste - dissolvable gums with clean and effective ingredients sold in stainless steel jars. Zero Waste. The product reduces waste and dangerous chemicals involved in the creation of traditional toothpaste.


The Team

We are a team of four international women representing Poland and Bulgaria. Equilibre is our common passion that brings together our feminine side along with the duty to preserve our precious home- planet Earth.We re commited to making Equilibre a brand that brings change.
