Sustainable


Sustainability is one of our leading company values. Nowadays, there are so many products to choose from wherever we go,all with plastic package around them. We don't realize the huge impact our shopping behaviour has on the environment, but the statistics shows that one garbage truck of plastic waste is produced every minute. What we aim for is to bring awareness to consumers and show them that every small step makes a difference. The products we are producing are contained within steel refillable jars and are 100% plastic-free.
