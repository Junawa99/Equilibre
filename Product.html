All our products


Customers decide to buy the Equilibre toothpaste because of its unique gum-like formula. Unlike normal toothpastes in the form of a gel, our product is in the shape of gum and when put in the mouth, it dissolves, and the customer can easily brush his teeth. The formula we offer is eco-friendly and plastic-free since there is no plastic packaging, instead the product is contained in a steel-jar, which can be refilled after the product is over. Moreover, our product is extremely convenient to bring with while travelling
